About TechStorm

TechStorm, organized by the SIT ACM Student Chapter, is a dynamic event featuring hands-on workshops and competitive challenges. This edition includes a Canva Design Workshop to enhance creative design skills, a Computer Assembly Workshop for hands-on PC building experience, and an intense Valorant eSports Tournament for gaming enthusiasts. Join us to learn, compete, and showcase your skills!

Who We Are?

TechStorm is proudly organized by the ACM SIT Student Chapter. Our dedicated team of students works tirelessly to bring together technology, innovation, and collaboration. With a shared vision for fostering technical excellence, we strive to create an event that inspires, educates, and challenges the brightest minds!

In Association With

SIT ACM Student Chapter

The SIT ACM Student Chapter is a community of tech enthusiasts dedicated to exploring computer science, coding, and innovation. It organizes workshops, hackathons, and technical events to help students enhance their skills, collaborate, and stay updated with industry trends.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our goal is to create an inclusive platform where students can learn, innovate, and excel in science, technology, and engineering through competitions and workshops.

Our Vision

Our goal is to become a leading tech fest that nurtures talent, inspires innovation, and creates a collaborative space for students to showcase their skills on a global stage.
